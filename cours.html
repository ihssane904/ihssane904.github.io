Present countinuous:

To make this tense we use am / are / is + verb + ing

so we never say: I am play, but I am playing. This is why it is necessary to learn by heart how to use the verb to be in the present .

  • For most verbs we just add ing :speak--> speaking
  • For verbs that end with e,we omit the e and add ing : dance--> dancing
  • For COV(consonant-vowel-consonant) verbs, we double the last consonant : put--> putting ;sit--> sitting ; run--> running.
  • when the stress is not on the last syllable, we do not doublethe consonant: listen--> listening

    • we use this tense:

      For actions happening now at the time of speaking.
    • I am listening to you.
    • I am paying attention
      For actions happening at the present time.
    • she is preparing for the exam.

    We use time expressions like now; at the moment; at the time being; this year and some verbs like listen; look exemple:I'm doing my research this year.

Indefinite Pronouns

some/ any / no / every + body/ one / thing/ where

    somebody, someone , something , somewhere

  • we use somebody, someone , something , somewhere to talk about an unspecified person, thing or place .we use these words in affirmative sentences .

    • Exemple

    -someone phoned you this morning .
    - look there is something on the floor.

    anybody, anyone , anything , anywhere

  • we use anybody, anyone , anything , anywhere to talk about an unspecified person, thing or place .we use these words in negative sentences and questions .

    • Exemple

    -I can't find my ball pen anywhere
    -Did anybody complain ?
    -we attended the party ,but we didn't eat anything

    nobody, no one , nothing , nowhere

  • we use nobody, no one , nothing , nowhere to talk about an unspecified person, thing or place .we use these words when the verb is affirmative but the sentence has a negative meaning .

    • Exemple

    - nobody / no one will leave the room
    -there is nothing I can do for you.
    -Poor boy! he's got nowhere to go .

    everybody, everyoneone , everythingthing , everywhere:

  • we use everybody, everyoneone , everythingthing , everywhere to talk about an unspecified person, thing or place .we use these words in affirmative sentences and questions

    Exemple

    -everybody 's here.
    -Is everything ok?
    - do these plants grow everywhere
